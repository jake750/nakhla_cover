Our Story

From Oasis Tradition to Modern Passive Cooling

Born in the palm groves of Gabès, Tunisia, NAKHLA COVER brings the ancient craft of palm frond weaving into contemporary architecture. Each piece is made from 100% natural Sâf and designed to protect interiors from intense heat while adding a warm, handcrafted statement to homes and studios.

Our covers are a sustainable alternative to plastic sunscreens, supporting local artisans and making every building feel both cooler and more connected to nature.
